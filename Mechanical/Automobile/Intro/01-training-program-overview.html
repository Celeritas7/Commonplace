Key Facts

  • 24 distinct modules cover engines, electricals, chassis, comfort systems, soft-skills.
  • Four BMW engines explicitly studied: N-42, N-52, N-62, M47 (last is diesel).
  • Two flagship vehicles for "complete vehicle" study: E60 and E65.
  • Hands-on practicals included starter-motor and alternator teardowns and brake/suspension/steering work.
  • Steering-geometry topics covered: Caster, Camber, Kingpin Inclination, Track width.
  • Customer base reaches beyond BMW — Rolls Royce, Land Rover, Volvo, Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Honda, JCB.
